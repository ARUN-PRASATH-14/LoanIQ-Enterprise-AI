CANARA BANK LOAN POLICIES 2026

1. Canara Bank Home Loan:
Interest Rate: 8.55% p.a. onwards.
Margin: 10% for loans up to Rs. 30 Lakhs, 20% for loans above Rs. 30 Lakhs.
Condition: Only for construction, purchase, or repair of a house/flat.

2. Canara Bank Personal Loan (Canara Budget):
Interest Rate: Starts at 10.95% p.a.
Maximum Amount: Up to Rs. 10 Lakhs.
Condition: Applicants must draw a net salary of at least Rs. 15,000 per month (Rs. 20,000 for metro areas).

3. Canara Bank Education Loan (IBA Model):
Interest Rate: Starts at 8.60% p.a.
Condition: Loans granted for recognized courses in India and abroad. Up to Rs. 7.5 Lakhs without collateral based on parents' co-obligation.

4. Canara Bank Farmer Loan (Canara Kisan / KCC):
Interest Rate: 7.00% p.a. (subject to 3% subvention for prompt repayment).
Coverage: Loans for crop cultivation, post-harvest expenses, and working capital for maintenance of farm assets.
