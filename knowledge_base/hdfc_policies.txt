HDFC BANK LOAN POLICIES 2026

1. HDFC Home Loan:
Interest Rate: 8.75% p.a. onwards depending on credit score and loan amount.
Processing Fee: Up to 0.50% of the loan amount or Rs. 3000, whichever is higher.
Tenure: Maximum up to 30 years.
Condition: Applicants must be aged between 21 and 65 years.

2. HDFC Personal Loan:
Interest Rate: Starts at 10.50% p.a.
Maximum Amount: Up to Rs. 40 Lakhs for salaried individuals.
Approval Time: Pre-approved loans disbursed in 10 seconds.
Condition: Minimum net monthly income of Rs. 25,000 required.

3. HDFC Education Loan (Credila):
Interest Rate: 9.50% p.a. onwards.
Maximum Amount: Up to Rs. 30 Lakhs for domestic, no upper cap for international (depends on collateral).
Repayment: Flexible repayment options; EMI starts after the course duration plus grace period of 6 months.

4. HDFC Farmer Loan (Agri Loan / KCC):
Interest Rate: Approx 9.00% p.a. depending on the crop and season.
Repayment: Custom repayment schedules aligned with harvest and marketing of the crop.
Collateral: Mortgage of agricultural land required for loans above Rs. 1.6 Lakhs.
