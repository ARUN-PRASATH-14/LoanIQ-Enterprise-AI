ICICI BANK LOAN POLICIES 2026

1. ICICI Home Loan:
Interest Rate: Starts from 8.75% p.a. for salaried profiles.
Tenure: Maximum up to 30 years.
Condition: Applicants must have a minimum employment history of 2 years.

2. ICICI Personal Loan:
Interest Rate: Starts at 10.75% p.a.
Maximum Amount: Up to Rs. 50 Lakhs depending on credit profile.
Processing Fee: Up to 2.50% of the loan amount.
Condition: Minimum CIBIL score of 720 recommended for quick processing.

3. ICICI Education Loan:
Interest Rate: 9.75% p.a. onwards.
Collateral: Collateral-free loans up to Rs. 20 Lakhs for students admitted to premier Indian institutes (IIMs, IITs).
Maximum Amount: Up to Rs. 1 Crore for international education with collateral.

4. ICICI Farmer Loan (Tractor Loan & KCC):
Interest Rate: Approx 10.00% p.a. for tractor and farm equipment loans.
Repayment: Fast processing with repayment schedules varying from 12 to 60 months based on cropping patterns.
