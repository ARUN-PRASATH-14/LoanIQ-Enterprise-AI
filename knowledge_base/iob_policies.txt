INDIAN OVERSEAS BANK (IOB) LOAN POLICIES 2026

1. IOB Home Loan (Subha Gruha):
Interest Rate: Starts at 8.60% p.a.
LTV Ratio: 90% for loans up to Rs. 30 Lakhs, 80% for loans between Rs. 30 to 75 Lakhs.
Condition: Applicants must be individuals with assured steady income.

2. IOB Personal Loan (Royal):
Interest Rate: Starts at 11.50% p.a.
Maximum Amount: Rs. 15 Lakhs or 15 times gross monthly salary (whichever is lower).
Condition: Only for confirmed employees of reputed companies/government, with minimum 3 years of service.

3. IOB Education Loan (Vidya Jyoti):
Interest Rate: 9.00% p.a. for domestic studies.
Condition: Loan covering up to 100% of fees for premier institutions. Margin of 5% required for loans above Rs. 4 Lakhs.

4. IOB Agriculture Loan:
Interest Rate: 7.00% p.a. for short term crop loans.
Coverage: Includes financing for seeds, fertilizers, and minor irrigation projects under Priority Sector Lending.
