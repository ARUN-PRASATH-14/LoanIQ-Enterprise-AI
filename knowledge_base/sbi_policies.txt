STATE BANK OF INDIA (SBI) LOAN POLICIES 2026

1. SBI Home Loan:
Interest Rate: Starts from 8.50% p.a.
Processing Fee: 0.35% of the loan amount (plus applicable GST).
Tenure: Up to 30 years.
LTV Ratio: Up to 90% for loans under Rs. 30 Lakhs.
Condition: Minimum CIBIL score of 700 required for the lowest rate.

2. SBI Personal Loan (Xpress Credit):
Interest Rate: Starts from 11.15% p.a.
Maximum Amount: Up to Rs. 20 Lakhs or 24 times NMI.
Tenure: Up to 6 years (72 months).
Condition: Must maintain salary account with SBI. DTI should not exceed 50%.

3. SBI Education Loan (Student Loan Scheme):
Interest Rate: 8.65% p.a. (0.50% concession for girl students).
Collateral: No collateral or third-party guarantee required for loans up to Rs. 7.5 Lakhs.
Maximum Amount: Rs. 50 Lakhs for studies in India, Rs. 1.5 Crores for overseas.

4. SBI Agriculture/Farmer Loan (Kisan Credit Card - KCC):
Interest Rate: 7.00% p.a. for short-term crop loans up to Rs. 3 Lakhs.
Prompt Repayment Subvention: 3.00% p.a. incentive for prompt repayment, making the effective rate 4.00% p.a.
Collateral: No collateral for KCC limit up to Rs. 1.6 Lakhs.
